Pricing for verified Ethereum state feeds.

Paxiom sells scoped access to proof-labeled Ethereum predicates. Start with public schemas and sample records, move into a paid pilot, then expand into production feeds and custom predicates.

Paxiom does not sell custody, execution, or investment advice. Pricing is for authenticated data access, proof context, commitment lookup, and support.

Enterprise And Custom Predicates

Large protocols, risk vendors, custody teams, and ecosystem partners can sponsor custom predicates. A custom predicate turns a protocol-specific state question into a reusable Paxiom feed.

Line item Typical range What it covers
Custom predicate build $15K-$75K one time Storage layout work, decoder, proof adapter, tests, record schema, and integration support.
Enterprise access $50K-$250K+ annually Dedicated scope, higher limits, priority roadmap input, SLA terms, and custom support.
Exclusivity Custom Not included by default. Exclusive predicates require a much larger agreement.

What Determines Price

Dimension What changes
Predicate scope Which facts the token can read: storage_equals, uniswap_v3_slot0, and future decision predicates.
Subject scope Which pools, contracts, markets, accounts, or protocol objects are included.
Verification level mpt_verified today, AO-committed next, recursive aggregation later.
Freshness Finalized records first. Safe and latest-state products require stricter latency and risk terms.
Volume and history Requests per day, burst rate, retention window, and commitment lookup depth.

First Product Benchmark

Current finalized uniswap_v3_slot0 proof generation measured p50 0.69s and p95 1.22s across 10 local runs on April 26, 2026. Production deployment will publish predicate-level latency as part of the feed documentation.
