How Paxiom turns Ethereum state into subscriber-grade facts.

Paxiom packages verifiable claims about Ethereum into API records that trading, risk, monitoring, and settlement systems can consume without trusting a hosted indexer as the final source of truth.

The Consumer Workflow

1. Choose a predicateA predicate is the exact claim a subscriber wants: pool state, storage value, event inclusion, account condition, or a higher-level protocol assertion.
2. Paxiom verifies the factThe current feed verifies Ethereum storage proofs locally against the block state root, decodes protocol-specific values, and labels the verification level.
3. The feed emits a recordEach record carries the value plus block number, state root, proof system, proof hash, Paxiom commitment, feed hash, and custody label.
4. Subscribers act or archiveDownstream systems can gate alerts, store evidence, compare facts across feeds, and look up commitments later.
5. Proof depth increasesThe roadmap moves from MPT-verified facts to AO-committed receipts, recursive aggregation, and eventually live ZK proofs for Ethereum inclusion and state continuity.

Verification Levels

Paxiom is explicit about proof depth. Early feeds are useful because they already verify Ethereum storage openings; later feeds become stronger as state roots and historical continuity are proven with less operator trust.

Today, mpt_verified records verify against finalized Ethereum state roots sourced from external Ethereum infrastructure. That root source is the remaining trust boundary. AO commitments, recursive aggregation, and beaconless proving are the planned steps for reducing it.

mpt_verifiedStorage or account data is verified against an Ethereum state root using a Merkle Patricia proof.
ao_committedThe verified fact is committed through an AO proof-corpus process for shared coordination and retrieval.
zk_inclusionKeccak, RLP, and MPT inclusion move into a proving backend so the inclusion check is compactly verifiable.
recursive_historySegment proofs aggregate toward a genesis-to-present corpus, reducing reliance on trusted state-root beacons.

Latency And Fit

The first Paxiom package is strongest where evidence matters: audit trails, signal review, risk controls, post-trade analysis, and monitors that can tolerate finalized-block latency. Safe and latest block modes are future product tiers with different settlement assumptions.

Best first fit

Evidence and monitoring workflows where a replayable proof-labeled fact is more valuable than raw speed.

Measure per predicate

Paxiom should publish observed latency by predicate, subject, block mode, and RPC/prover backend.

Not magic speed

MPT proof fetch and verification have a floor. Buyers should match feeds to their decision window.

What Paxiom Does Not Do

Paxiom does not custody funds, execute trades, provide investment advice, or promise that a feed consumer will make profitable decisions. It provides proof-labeled Ethereum facts that subscribers can verify and use according to their own risk policy.
